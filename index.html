Caffeine, Stress & Lifestyle Factors

CoffeeLens Team: Nichol Flowers, Oleksii Lavrenin, Abhay Naik, Bryan Park

Goals: This visualization aims to reveal broad patterns in caffeine use across diverse populations and to offer a clear way to explore how these patterns differ among groups of people.

Intended Audience: This visualization is for anyone who is curious about how their caffeine routine fits into broader patterns. Whether your drink is tea, espresso, soda, or matcha, it offers a way to explore how caffeine connects to stress, sleep, and age among people around the world.

Data Sources: synthetic_coffee_health_10000.csv and coffee_types.csv
